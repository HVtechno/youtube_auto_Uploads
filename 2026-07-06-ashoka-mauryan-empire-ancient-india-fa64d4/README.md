# Ashoka’s Stone Messages to an Empire

In the Mauryan Empire, Emperor Ashoka used rock and pillar edicts to speak directly about justice, restraint, compassion, and care for people and animals. These public inscriptions show an ancient experiment in making ethical governance visible across a vast, diverse realm. #AncientWisdom #MauryanEmpire #Ashoka #ForgottenHistory #EthicalLeadership
