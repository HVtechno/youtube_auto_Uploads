# The Ancient Council Built for the Future

The Haudenosaunee Confederacy used a council system shaped by consensus, clan mothers, and responsibility to future generations. Its Great Law of Peace offers a grounded example of governance designed to prevent rash decisions and protect community balance. #AncientWisdom #IndigenousHistory #Haudenosaunee #Governance #ForgottenHistory
